SWORD

How to play


  1. You get 6 attempts to guess the word
  2. Type your guess
  3. Click ENTER
  4. If the nth letter of your guess is same as the nth letter of the word,it will be highlighted in green
  5. If the letter is present in the word, it will be highlighted in yellow
  6. If the letter is absent, it will be highlighted in black
  7. Continue Steps 2 to 5 till all the cells are green
  8. If you fail to do so in 6 attempts, you will lose and the answer will be displayed at the top of your screen
